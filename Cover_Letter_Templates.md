## Customization Checklist

Before sending any cover letter, customize these elements:

**Company Research (Essential):**
- [ ] Hiring manager name (LinkedIn search or company directory)
- [ ] Recent company news/product launches (past 3 months)
- [ ] Company blog posts or technical articles
- [ ] Specific team you're applying to
- [ ] Company values and culture

**Letter Customization:**
- [ ] Replace all [Company Name] placeholders
- [ ] Replace all [Hiring Manager Name] placeholders
- [ ] Add specific company initiative/project reference
- [ ] Mention relevant technology from job description
- [ ] Connect your skills to specific job requirements
- [ ] Add genuine enthusiasm for company's mission

**Final Checks:**
- [ ] Proofread for typos and grammar
- [ ] Verify all metrics are accurate
- [ ] Ensure letter is 1 page maximum
- [ ] Use company-specific keywords from job posting
- [ ] Maintain professional but enthusiastic tone
- [ ] Include correct contact information
- [ ] Attach resume and portfolio link

---

## Quick Customization Guide

**For Startups:**
- Emphasize adaptability, fast learning, wearing multiple hats
- Mention specific technologies in their stack
- Show excitement for their product/mission
- Reference recent funding or growth milestones

**For Big Tech:**
- Highlight scalability and production experience
- Mention relevant tech stack (AWS, distributed systems)
- Reference specific products or teams
- Emphasize impact metrics (90% accuracy, 40% reduction)

**For Research Organizations:**
- Lead with statistical rigor and novel methodology
- Emphasize Bayesian modeling and experimental design
- Reference publications or academic work
- Highlight contribution to knowledge

**For Healthcare/Finance:**
- Emphasize accuracy metrics (99.12%, 100% precision)
- Highlight attention to detail and validation
- Mention regulatory awareness if relevant
- Focus on business impact and risk reduction
